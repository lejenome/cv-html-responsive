Merry Christmas 2013 - Hacking the Christmas Card

This was my quick holiday hack for 2013. Recipients were mailed a christmas card with a unique 4 color combination on the front. Inside of each card was a link to a webapp hosted on a heroku repository - "merry-christmas.herokuapp.com". On that website, recipients were expected to enter their unique 4 color code and a custom christmas card was pulled up for them on the webpage.
